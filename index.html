01 — About

A career built around judgment, trust and the people decisions that shape companies.

Trained as a psychologist at Universidad del Norte, Ingrid spent her early career inside the firms that set the standard for executive search in the region — Spencer Stuart, Hays — and later as an independent and boutique consultant at OBLIVIEM, before stepping into in-house talent leadership at Alpina and, today, TP.

What clients describe in their own words: professionalism, integrity, and a manager colleagues look to for career guidance. The work is HR consulting, executive search and organizational development — but the underlying craft is the same: understanding people well enough to put the right ones in the right roles, at moments that matter.
